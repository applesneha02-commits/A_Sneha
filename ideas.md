# Sneha's Portfolio — Design Direction

## Design Philosophy: "Vibrant Growth"

**Chosen Approach:** A lively, colorful, and energetic portfolio that mirrors Sneha's dynamic work in social media growth and content strategy.

### Core Design Principles
1. **Chromatic Energy** — Multi-color palette (coral, teal, amber, lime) that feels modern and approachable, never flat or corporate
2. **Growth Narrative** — Visual progression from hero through skills, experience, and projects that builds momentum
3. **Asymmetric Layouts** — Offset grids and staggered sections avoid centered monotony; creates visual interest and guides the eye
4. **Micro-interactions** — Hover effects, scroll reveals, and subtle animations that celebrate user engagement

### Color Philosophy
- **Primary Accent:** Vibrant Coral (#FF6B5B) — warm, energetic, inviting
- **Secondary Accent:** Teal (#00D9D9) — fresh, modern, growth-oriented
- **Tertiary Accent:** Amber (#FFB627) — optimistic, creative energy
- **Quaternary Accent:** Lime (#7FFF00) — bold, attention-grabbing
- **Background:** Clean white (#FFFFFF) with subtle gradient overlays
- **Text:** Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) for readability
- **Accents on dark sections:** Soft cream (#F5F5F5)

**Emotional Intent:** Professional yet approachable. Colors signal growth, creativity, and forward momentum—perfect for a digital marketer scaling brands.

### Layout Paradigm
- **Hero Section:** Full-width asymmetric layout with staggered text and animated gradient background
- **Skills Grid:** 2-3 column layout with card hover effects and color-coded categories
- **Experience Timeline:** Diagonal staggered cards with connecting lines
- **Projects Showcase:** Masonry-inspired grid with overlapping cards
- **Call-to-Action:** Sticky footer with gradient buttons

### Signature Elements
1. **Animated Gradient Orbs** — Subtle floating shapes in the background (inspired by reference but with our color palette)
2. **Color-Coded Tags** — Each skill/tool tagged with a specific color from the palette
3. **Diagonal Dividers** — SVG wave/diagonal cuts between sections for visual flow
4. **Stat Callouts** — Bold numbers highlighting growth metrics (500+ connections, 2K+ impressions, 40% follower growth)

### Interaction Philosophy
- **Hover States:** Cards lift with shadow, text color shifts to accent colors
- **Scroll Reveals:** Elements fade in and slide up as they enter viewport
- **Button Feedback:** Smooth scale and color transitions on click/hover
- **Navigation:** Smooth scroll anchors with active state indicators

### Animation Guidelines
- **Entrance Animations:** Staggered fade-in + slide-up (300ms ease-out) for grouped elements
- **Hover Effects:** 150ms smooth color/scale transitions
- **Scroll Reveals:** 400ms cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1) for a snappy feel
- **Micro-interactions:** Keep all transitions under 200ms for responsiveness

### Typography System
- **Display Font:** "Poppins" (bold, geometric) — for hero, section titles, stat callouts
- **Body Font:** "Inter" (clean, modern) — for descriptions, body text
- **Accent Font:** "Playfair Display" (elegant serif) — for section subtitles and highlights

**Hierarchy:**
- H1: 3.5rem, Poppins 800, letter-spacing -0.02em
- H2: 2.5rem, Poppins 700, letter-spacing -0.01em
- H3: 1.5rem, Poppins 600
- Body: 1rem, Inter 400, line-height 1.6
- Small: 0.875rem, Inter 500, color muted

### Brand Essence
**One-liner:** "A social media strategist who scales brands through data-driven content and authentic engagement."

**Personality Adjectives:** Strategic, Creative, Energetic

### Brand Voice
- **Headlines:** Bold, action-oriented, growth-focused
- **CTAs:** Conversational, inviting, clear
- **Microcopy:** Friendly, professional, no corporate jargon

**Example Lines:**
- "From 100 to 500+ connections — here's how I scale brands"
- "Let's build something that converts"

### Wordmark & Logo
- **Logo Concept:** A stylized "S" with an upward arrow integrated into the letterform, symbolizing growth
- **Color:** Gradient from coral to teal
- **Style:** Modern, geometric, memorable

### Signature Brand Color
**Vibrant Coral (#FF6B5B)** — Unmistakably Sneha's. Used for CTAs, highlights, and key visual elements.

## Reference Inspiration
- **Layout:** Asymmetric hero, numbered sections, diagonal dividers (inspired by ganeshk.vercel.app)
- **Unique Twist:** Vibrant multi-color palette instead of monochrome; focus on growth metrics and social media expertise

## Content Structure
1. **Hero** — Name, tagline, CTA, animated background
2. **About** — Brief intro with key stats
3. **Skills** — Color-coded categories (Social Media, Content, Tools, Analytics)
4. **Experience** — Timeline of roles and achievements
5. **Projects** — Showcase of social media campaigns and growth projects
6. **Certifications** — Professional credentials
7. **Contact** — Call-to-action with contact details
